CORAL REEFS SUPPORT MORE THAN 4000 SPECIES OF FISH, 800 SPECIES OF HARD CORAL, AND HUNDRED OF OTHER SPECIES.

Biodiversity is dependent on coral reefs. When the inital smaller fish die, the fish that consume the smaller fish will die off, and soon our fish supply is gone.

CORAL REEFS ARE ESPECIALLY IMPORTANT TO ACT AS NATURAL BARRIERS AGAINST STORM EVENTS LIKE HURRICANES, TYPOONS, AND EVEN TSUNAMIS.

Without coral reefs, storms will have a more tremendous effect.
